Work with us


At The Magic Beans, we believe that a great coffee shop is built by a passionate team.
Our staff members are enthusiastic about what they do, and they love helping each other out.
We foster a positive and fun work environment where everyone feels valued and supported.

If you're looking to join a team that works together to create delightful experiences for our customers, we'd love to have you on board!



Current Openings:

We are seeking an enthusiastic and dedicated Barista to join our team. As a Barista, you will play a key role in providing exceptional customer service and crafting high-quality coffee beverages. If you have a passion for coffee and enjoy interacting with people, we'd love to meet you!

Responsibilities:

  • Prepare and serve a variety of coffee drinks and other beverages.
  • Maintain a clean and organized workspace.
  • Engage with customers, take orders, and handle transactions.
  • Assist in inventory management and ordering supplies.
Qualifications:
  • Previous experience as a Barista or in a customer service role is a plus.
  • Knowledge of coffee brewing techniques and familiarity with coffee shop equipment.
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Attention to detail and commitment to quality.
What We Offer:
  • Competitive hourly wage plus tips.
  • Training and development opportunities.
  • Friendly and supportive team environment.

We're on the lookout for a talented Pastry Chef to become a key player in our team. Your main role will be to create a variety of delightful pastries, desserts, and baked goods that our customers will love. With your passion for baking and innovative culinary skills, you will help elevate our menu and bring joy to those who visit our coffee shop.

Responsibilities:

  • Prepare and bake a variety of pastries, cakes, cookies, and other baked goods.
  • Develop new recipes and menu items that align with our coffee shop's brand and customer preferences.
  • Maintain a clean and organized kitchen workspace.
  • Adhere to food safety and sanitation guidelines.
Qualifications:
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and handle multiple tasks.
  • Culinary degree or relevant certification preferred.
  • Strong knowledge of baking techniques, ingredients, and equipment.
What We Offer:
  • Competitive hourly wage.
  • Training and development opportunities.
  • Friendly and supportive team environment.



Working at The Magic Beans


Friendly Atmosphere: We believe that a positive work environment is key to happiness and productivity. Our team is like a family, supporting and encouraging each other every step of the way.

Opportunities for Growth: We value your career development and offer plenty of opportunities for advancement. Whether it's learning new skills, taking on more responsibilities, or pursuing leadership roles, we're here to help you achieve your goals.

Creative Freedom: Have a brilliant idea for a new coffee blend or a fun event? We're all ears! We love incorporating our team members' creativity and passion into our shop's offerings.

Community Engagement: We're not just a coffee shop; we're a community hub. You'll have the chance to connect with our loyal customers and participate in local events, making a real impact in our neighborhood.
